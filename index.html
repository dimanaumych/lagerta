We aspire to a holistic world model in which women equally create meanings and impact societal changes alongside men

Join us

Everything unexpected and tragic begins with marginalization. The exclusion of women from the role of creators of meanings that shape the general direction of societal development still exists. We

continue to live and strive for a world built mainly on men's model of values. We see this model as incomplete and offer to inform it with valuable meanings from conscious and educated women.

We don't aim to compete with men or displace them. What we want is to take our equal place next to them for the sake of the holistic world.
We are different, but we are equal.

LAGERTA is a public organization that supports the revival of women's elite in Ukraine and worldwide
